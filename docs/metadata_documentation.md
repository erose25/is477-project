# Metadata and Dataset Documentation

## World Bank Findex 2025 Dataset
### Source: 
- https://www.worldbank.org/en/publication/globalfindex/download-data

### Variable Definitions: 
- country: Country
- country_code: Country Code
- year: Year
- account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have an account
- bank_account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have an account with a bank of similar financial institution
- mobile_account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have a mobile money account
- digital_account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have a digitally-enabled account
