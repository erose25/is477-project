# Metadata and Dataset Documentation

## World Bank Findex 2025 Dataset
### Source: 
- https://www.worldbank.org/en/publication/globalfindex/download-data

### Variable Definitions: 
- country: Country
- country_code: Country Code
- year: Year
- account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have an account
- bank_account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have an account with a bank of similar financial institution
- mobile_account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have a mobile money account
- digital_account_pct: % of residents over 15 years of age that have a digitally-enabled account

## Poverty and Inequality Platform (PIP) Dataset
### Source: 
- https://pip.worldbank.org/poverty-calculator

### Variable Definitions: 
- country_code: ISO 3-digit country code (e.g., AGO, ALB, USA)
- country_name: Full name of the country or territory
- year: Year of the survey or observation (range: 1963–2024)
- poverty_headcount: Proportion of the population living below the national or international poverty line (typically $2.15/day in 2017 PPP for international lines). Values are expressed as a decimal (e.g., 0.2695 = 26.95%).
- gini: Gini coefficient measuring income or consumption inequality. Values range from 0 (perfect equality) to 1 (perfect inequality). Expressed as a decimal (e.g., 0.519 = 51.9).
